Ending the child mortality crisis one SMS at a time.
In Nigeria, over 118 of every 1,000 children die before their 5th birthday.
Why are so many children dying?
That's 700,000 children dying each year from PREVENTABLE causes.
That's 21x times higher than Russia
9x times higher than Brazil and 49x higher than Norway
Because mothers aren't breastfeeding
Breast milk includes an edible carbon copy of the mother’s immune system, which a baby’s body uses to protect itself before its own immune system develops. The baby's first vaccine.
It immunizes children from leading causes of death such as pneumonia and diarrhea and provides all required nutrients.
Here's why mothers aren't doing it.

Breastfeeding is a learned behavior.
99% of mothers can physically breastfeed, but they don’t know how.

Hospitals don’t employ lactation counselors and there is no education or emphasis on it within the community.

An SMS app to provide information and breastfeeding support for mothers.
Mwana is SMS-based to account for those without smartphones. Mothers can join simply by texting a given phone number.
The Mwana app is set up in a familiar format with clear instructions for mothers to learn with no experience.
We consult and work in tandem experts in lactation to write and verify that our tips will improve your experience.
If there are any questions not added to our system, message the team and we will answer your question.
Many of our users are not English-natives. We've decided to make Mwana available in English and Hausa so all mothers are welcome.
